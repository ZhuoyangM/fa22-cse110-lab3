First Team Meeting

Recorder: Wilson Irwin

Time: 10/03/2022 | Location: WLH 2001

Attendance List

  • Oliver Bolton
  • Dwayne Hastings
  • Pamela Higgins
  • Marlene Perez
  • Wilson Irwin

Agenda

  1. Every team member takes turns to do a self-introduction;
  2. Brainstorm to come up with an awesome group name;
  3. Select a team leader;
  4. Set up team rules;
  5. Assign tasks for each team member;
  6. Wrap up everything.

Comments

The meeting last for an hour, and the team finished most tasks listed in the agenda. For team name, the team adopted Oliver's idea that proposed the name Kingmakers. After a brief self-introduction session, the team selected Wilson as the team leader through anonymous vote.

One thing to notice is that some team members disagreed on certain rules that others proposed. Thus, in next meeting the team should finish setting up team rules first before moving on to the main contents of next meeting.
